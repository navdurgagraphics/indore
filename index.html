Frequently Asked Questions

What are your prices?

Our prices depend on the material, size, and quantity of the order. We offer very competitive and affordable rates. Please WhatsApp us with your requirements for a quick quote!

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time varies based on the order size. Small retail orders can often be completed the same day or within 24 hours. We will give you an estimated delivery time when you place your order.

Which file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept high-resolution files in CDR (CorelDRAW), PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), PSD (Adobe Photoshop), and high-quality JPEG/PNG formats. For best results, CDR or PDF is preferred.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No, there is no minimum order quantity! We handle both single retail orders and large bulk orders with the same dedication and quality.

Can you help with designing?

Yes! We have an expert design team that can help you create professional and attractive designs for your printing needs. Just share your idea with us on WhatsApp.
